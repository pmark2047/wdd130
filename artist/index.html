Purpose

I've been playing music since I was 7 years old when I started taking piano lessons. Growing up I found it as a unique way to express myself in a way that words couldn't and I loved to write arrangements of church hymns and other songs that I loved. Over time I've had lots of people ask me for the sheet music of my own arrangements and compositions. This site is so that I can share that music and expression for those who want to listen and learn the material I've written throughout my life. Over time, I plan to fill this site with videos, mp3s, sheet music, and chords so that my music can be out for all to experience and enjoy.
